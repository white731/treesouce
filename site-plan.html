Overview

Purpose

I want to create an online rental marketplace where people can rent everyday household goods to and from people in their area - like Airbnb, but for everything else.

Have you ever wanted to try something new like play the guitar or ride a mountain bike but wanted to test it out first before spending a ton of money? With my website, you can do just that. Search for items in your area that are for rent and try them out for a fraction of the cost of buying them.

Have you ever bought something that you've only used once and is now collecting dust in your garage? With my website, you can turn those items into cash-generating assets by listing for them rent online.

There is currently no centralized location on the internet where people can securely rent to and from people in their community. I want to change that.

Audience

My audience will consist of Millenials that now entering into the world of adulthood. They're having kids, buying homes and realizing the realities of what it takes to be adults. But because we live in a new world different than our parents, they're part of the "share" generation. They're used to staying in other peoples homes (airbnb) or riding in a strangers car (uber). They'll be comfortable renting a lawn mower from the neighbor or renting a chainsaw to cut down a big tree instead of having to own these things like their parents did.

Content

Home page

Hey Neighbor! Welcome to the neighborhood! We’re a community of people who are all about sharing what we have with our neighbors.

We live in a world that shares a lot (AND THAT’S GOOD!). We share our experience, our knowledge (thanks internet), our cars and even our homes! But most people don’t share their “Stuff”. Stuff like a bike, guitar, hammer, camera, game console… the list goes on and on. We all have some of these things but most don’t have all, so let’s share!

Borrowing

How are you supposed borrow stuff? Hey Neighbor! That’s what we’re here for! We’ve created a neighborhood of borrowers and lenders who can now find each other in this share driven world. It’s easy. Just head over to “The Neighborhood” and start searching for “Stuff”. If you can’t find what you’re looking for then “ask a neighbor” and more than likely someone will reach out.

Sharing

What if I want to share stuff? If you have something you own that you want to share with others in your community you can post it right here in the neighborhood. Members of your community will get to see it and eventually borrow it. You can choose to charge for the things you share or you can share it for free. It’s up to you!

We've got you covered

Sometimes sharing things can be a little scary. We want community members to feel safe and secure as they share the things they own with others. When you share an item with another you can choose to have it insured for a small price or request that the neighbor who is borrowing it puts up the full value of the item up front in case it gets broken or stolen. We also rate our members after each share so that other members can barrow and lend with confidence.

Join the Neighborhood

Joining the neighborhood is super easy, just sign up using the form below. By becoming a part of the neighborhood you’ll get to view all items that are available from neighbors just like yourself. Most communities have hundreds of items available to share with one another. Another benefit of becoming a member of the neighborhood is that YOU can start sharing your stuff too. After you sign up you’ll be guided through the process of how to list items to share. Note that there are rules and stipulations that surround what can be listed to share. See below for more details.

Sharing Policies

Here is some content that is ABSOLUTELY prohibited from listing on our site. Adult Products, Alcohol, Animals, Body Parts and bodily fluids, Jobs/Gigs/Services (This includes any form of service Including but not limited to cleaning services, massage services, hair cuts, modeling, etc.), money or any form of currency (You cannot lend money or borrow money on our site. IT IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED), Human Exploitation and Sexual Services, any form of edible items, medical or healthcare items, drugs of any form or type whether legal or illegal. Any items that are not “Family Friendly” (we are neighborhood sharing with members of the community who in some cases have families. All listings must be appropriate for children). Last of all, you may not sell weapons of any kind on the site. If you repeatedly list items that violate our policies, we may take additional action on your account. We reserve the right to reject, approve, or remove any item for any reason at any time, in our sole discretion.
